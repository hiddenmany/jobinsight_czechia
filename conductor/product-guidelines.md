# Communication Tone
- **Professional & Authoritative:** All communications must be factual, objective, and strictly data-driven to build trust with HR professionals and business executives.
- **Action-Oriented & Direct:** Focus on delivering high-impact insights concisely. Prioritize "What you need to know" and "What to do next."

# Visual Identity & UX
- **Modern & Interactive Design:** The user interface should utilize a clean layout (Streamlit) with a professional color palette. Interaction should be intuitive, balancing accessibility with visual polish.

# Language Standards
- **Czech Primary:** The user interface and market reports are primarily in Czech to ensure maximum accessibility for local business owners and recruitment teams.

# Data Ethics & Privacy
- **Transparent & Detailed Reporting:** Where possible, provide specific details about market shifts and hiring activity, including naming companies if the data is public and relevant.
- **Contextual Intelligence:** AI-generated commentary should provide deeper insights into *why* trends are occurring, adding value beyond raw numbers.

# AI Generation Principles (Gemini)
- **Data-Backed Verification:** Every insight generated by the AI must be verifiable against specific data points extracted by the scraper to prevent hallucinations.
- **Executive Summary Format:** Use bullet points, bold text, and clear headings to ensure key takeaways are immediately visible to busy professionals.
