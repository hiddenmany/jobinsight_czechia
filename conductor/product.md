# Initial Concept
Czech Market Intelligence: Actionable market insights for HR professionals and employers with advanced LLM insights.

# Product Vision
To become the definitive source of real-time labor market intelligence in the Czech Republic, empowering HR professionals and business leaders with data-driven transparency.

# Target Audience
- **HR Professionals & Tech Recruiters:** Who need precise salary benchmarks to remain competitive in a tight labor market.
- **Business Owners & Executives:** Who require strategic insights into market trends and competitor activity to plan growth.

# Core Value Proposition
- **Salary Transparency:** Solving the lack of clarity in Czech job listings by providing robust, multi-portal salary data by role and seniority.
- **Technology Tracking:** Monitoring the rapid shifts in technical skill premiums and stack modernity (Legacy vs. Modern vs. Stable).
- **Cost-Effective Research:** Replacing slow, expensive traditional market research with automated, AI-driven weekly intelligence.

# Key Features
- **Real-Time Benchmarking:** Automated extraction and analysis of salary ranges from major portals (Jobs.cz, Prace.cz, StartupJobs, etc.).
- **Modernity Analysis:** Semantic analysis of job descriptions to determine tech stack modernity and skill demand.
- **Adaptive Taxonomy:** Automated technical keyword discovery using statistical analysis and LLM verification to ensure classification accuracy as the market evolves.
- **AI-Powered Insights:** 
    - Automated weekly summary reports generated by Gemini AI.
    - Predictive "Market Pulse" alerts to highlight emerging trends in regional hubs like Prague, Brno, and Ostrava.

# Geographic Scope
- **National:** Comprehensive coverage of the Czech Republic labor market.
- **Regional Hubs:** Specific focus on high-activity areas including Prague, Brno, and Ostrava.
